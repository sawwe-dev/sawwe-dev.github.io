Brief biography

Stephen Hawking was a British physicist and scientific popularizer (born January 8, 1942 and died March 14, 2018 at age 76) best known for: Being the first scientist to offer a cosmological theory explained from the confluence of theory general relativity and quantum mechanics.

Most important awards

  • 2006 Copley Medal from the Royal Society
  • 2009 Presidential Medal of Freedom (America's highest civillian honour)
  • 2013 Russian Special Fundamental Physics Prize
